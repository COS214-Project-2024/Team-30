void Thieves::accessDamage ( shared_ptr< Building > buildingType)
overridevirtual

Calculates and applies theft damage to a specified building.

This function simulates a theft incident by randomly determining its severity (minor, moderate, or major) and calculating the corresponding damage. It then applies the calculated damage to the specified building and notifies the citizens within that building of the emergency.

Parameters
buildingTypeA shared pointer to the Building object that will take damage from the theft.
