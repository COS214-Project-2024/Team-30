Updates the economic state based on the current status of the Government.

Updates the economic state based on the Government's current population and employment data.

This method is called when the Government notifies its observers of changes. The Economy observer will assess the economic factors in Government and adjust accordingly.

Parameters
gPointer to the Government object.

This implementation displays a message indicating that the economy is adapting to support the current population. It then calls calculateEmploymentRate on the Government object to adjust the employment rate, reflecting the current state of the economy. Finally, it outputs revised city information.

Parameters
gPointer to the Government object that provides the current economic and population data.
