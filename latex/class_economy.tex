\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\doxylink{class_concrete}{Concrete} observer class that monitors and updates the economic state of the city. 

The {\ttfamily \doxylink{class_economy}{Economy}} class inherits from {\ttfamily \doxylink{class_city_growth_observer}{City\+Growth\+Observer}} and implements the {\ttfamily update} method to respond to changes in the cityâ€™s economy. This observer may adjust various economic indicators within the {\ttfamily \doxylink{class_government}{Government}} object to reflect current economic conditions.

Updates the economic state based on the current status of the \doxylink{class_government}{Government}. 

Updates the economic state based on the \doxylink{class_government}{Government}\textquotesingle{}s current population and employment data.

This method is called when the {\ttfamily \doxylink{class_government}{Government}} notifies its observers of changes. The {\ttfamily \doxylink{class_economy}{Economy}} observer will assess the economic factors in {\ttfamily \doxylink{class_government}{Government}} and adjust accordingly.
